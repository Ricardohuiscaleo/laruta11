# La Ruta 11 - Product Overview

## Purpose
La Ruta 11 is a comprehensive food truck management system for a Chilean burger restaurant. It provides a complete digital ecosystem for managing orders, inventory, customer interactions, and business operations across multiple touchpoints.

## Value Proposition
- **Multi-channel ordering**: Customers can order through web app with real-time menu updates
- **Integrated POS system**: Complete cashier/admin interface for order management and inventory control
- **Business intelligence**: Analytics, financial projections, and inventory tracking
- **Customer engagement**: Loyalty programs, cashback system, contests, and live streaming integration
- **Operational efficiency**: Automated inventory management, recipe costing, and purchase recommendations

## Key Features

### Customer-Facing (app.laruta11.cl)
- Progressive Web App (PWA) for mobile ordering
- Real-time menu with dynamic pricing and availability
- Product customization with extras and notes
- Combo deals with flexible product selection
- Multiple payment methods (TUU integration, transfers, cash)
- Loyalty rewards and cashback system
- Order tracking and notifications
- Product reviews and ratings
- Contest participation with live streaming

### Admin/Cashier System (caja.laruta11.cl)
- Point of Sale (POS) interface for in-person orders
- Order management dashboard with real-time updates
- Inventory management with automatic stock calculations
- Recipe management with ingredient tracking
- Purchase recommendations based on inventory levels
- Financial projections and analytics
- Product catalog management
- User management and order history
- Combo creation and management
- Notification system for new orders

### Landing Page (laruta11.cl)
- Public-facing website with business information
- Location and hours display
- Menu preview
- Contact information

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Customers**: People ordering food from La Ruta 11 food truck
- **Cashiers**: Staff taking orders and processing payments
- **Administrators**: Business owners managing operations and inventory
- **Kitchen Staff**: Preparing orders based on recipes and available ingredients

### Use Cases
- **Quick ordering**: Customers browse menu and place orders for pickup
- **Inventory control**: Admin tracks ingredient levels and receives purchase alerts
- **Financial planning**: Business owners analyze sales data and create projections
- **Customer retention**: Loyalty program encourages repeat purchases
- **Operational efficiency**: Automated stock calculations reduce waste and stockouts
- **Contest engagement**: Live streaming contests drive customer participation and brand awareness
